Welcome to the Haukai Restaurant.

We are a restaurant located in Kerikeri, in the Bay of Islands of New Zealand. Our restaurant specialises in Maori Kai, and other food reflecting the New Zealand experience.

We are small family business, and wish to have our customers experience Te Ao Māori with the use of karakia for blessing of our food.
